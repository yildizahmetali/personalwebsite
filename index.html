About Me

Software Engineer at Honeywell, pursuing an M.S. in Computer Science at Georgia Tech. Passionate about full-stack development and building real-world solutions through code.

I’ve led and contributed to impactful projects ranging from AI-powered grading tools to scalable web platforms and mentorship systems. With a strong focus on full-stack development, I blend backend logic, intuitive frontend design, and cloud infrastructure to create seamless, user-focused experiences that solve real-world problems.

Outside of coding, I’m committed to helping others grow. I mentor aspiring professionals through resume reviews, interview coaching, and career advice—sharing what I’ve learned to open doors for others. I also create content on Instagram to make career guidance accessible, and I recharge through soccer, travel, and exploring life beyond the screen.
